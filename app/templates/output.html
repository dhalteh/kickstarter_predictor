About the Kickstarter Campaign Predictor

To all you aspiring Kickstarter campaigners, are you curious as to how your project will perform before starting?

This application leverages data from nearly 21,000 past campaigns to help you gauge the likelihood of your project's success! Using a Random Forest that achieves a test Accuracy of 80% and a test AUC of 0.884, this application provides a good estimate of your campaign's probability of success based on a number of important listed above.

After inputting your information, please hit "Enter" to generate your prediction. Good luck!
